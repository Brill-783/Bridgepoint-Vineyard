WOMEN’SMINISTRY

VISION

To live life with purpose, diligence, exemplary, virtuous Christian women. Prov. 31

VALUES

✦ Hospitality To empower women to seek a higher level of Spiritual maturity in Christ through knowledge of biblical principles.

✦ To create an atmosphere that fosters fellowship, support, encouragement and unity among women.

✦ To provide instruction and teaching regarding the Christian woman's responsibility according to principles found in Titus 2 vs 3-5

PRIORITIES

✦ To utilize our gifts and talents to edify the body of Christ

✦ To empower and effectively minister to the needs of women

✦ To affirm and nurture, and strengthen women in the fellowship.

PRACTICES

✦ Evangelism

✦ Planning and hosting church programs

✦ Praying together as women and sharing the word of God

PROGRAMS: 2021

✦ Every Friday at 20hrs virtual prayers for 15-20 minutes
✦ One Saturday per month morning prayer and sharing the word 9.30 to 10.30hrs
✦ Visitation once every month
✦ Cookery once in every 3 months
✦ Every 6 months health talk

PROVERBS 31 WOMAN




✦ FAITH - serves God with all your heart, mind, and soul. (Proverbs 31: 26, Proverbs 31: 29 – 31, Matthew 22: 37, John 14: 15, Psalm 119: 15)
✦ MARRIAGE - a faithful bride. (Proverbs 31: 11- 12, Proverbs 31: 23, Proverbs 31: 28, 1 Peter 3, Ephesians 5, Genesis 2: 18)
✦ MOTHERING - nurtures children with the love of Christ, disciplines them with care and wisdom, and trains them in the way they should go. (Proverbs 31: 28, Proverbs 31: 26, Proverbs 22: 6, Deuteronomy 6, Luke 18: 16)
✦ HEALTH - takes care of the physical, mental, and spiritual health. (Proverbs 31: 14 – 15, Proverbs 31: 17, 1 Corinthians 6: 19, Genesis 1: 29, Daniel 1, Leviticus 11)
✦ SERVICE - serves husband, family, friends, and neighbors with a gentle and loving spirit. charitable too. (Proverbs 31: 12, Proverbs 31: 15, Proverbs 31: 20, 1 Corinthians 13: 13)
✦ STEWARDSHIP - a wise steward of the gifts God has given. spends money wisely. (Proverbs 31: 14, Proverbs 31: 16, Proverbs 31: 18, 1 Timothy 6: 10, Ephesians 5: 23, Deuteronomy 14: 22, Numbers 18: 26)
✦ INDUSTRY - industrious and works with willing hands. (Proverbs 31: 13, Proverbs 31: 16, Proverbs 31: 24, Proverbs 31: 31, Philippians 2: 14)
✦ HOMEMAKING - a good manager of the home. Creates an inviting atmosphere of warmth and love for her family and guests. Uses hospitality to minister to those around her. (Proverbs 31: 15, Proverbs 31: 20 – 22, Proverbs 31: 27, Titus 2: 5, 1 Peter 4: 9, Hebrews 13: 2)
✦ TIME - Spends time on that which is good. Dwelling on those things that please the Lord. (Proverbs 31: 13, Proverbs 31: 19, Proverbs 31: 27, Ecclesiastes 3, Proverbs 16: 9, Philippians 4:8 )
✦ BEAUTY - Creative and embraces beauty and godliness. Has the inner beauty that only comes from Christ. (Proverbs 31: 10, Proverbs 31: 21 – 22, Proverbs 31: 24 -25, Isaiah 61: 10, 1 Timothy 2: 9, 1 Peter 3: 1 – 6)
