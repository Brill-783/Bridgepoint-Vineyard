DOMINATE  YOUTH  MINISTRY

VISION

Inspiring and equipping young people to love God, love others and change the world. Equipping them to grow in their faith in Jesus Christ and igniting them with a passion and love for the lost and enjoying and sharing the love of God.

DOMINATE: have power and influence over.(according to Oxford languages) Hence the ministry will exist to lead the youths into the truth that God has given them power and influence over their lives. Genesis 1:27-28 Leading today's and tomorrows generation of youths into the truth of who they are


VALUES

✦ . The youth ministry is committed to and values prayer and fasting for the salvation of lost young people and for the empowerment of the holy spirit in taking the gospel to them

✦ We are committed to the word of God and laying it as a foundation of authority for life and ministry of young people.

✦ Believing that the church is the expression of the body of Christ and value partnering with the church in fulfilling the great commission of Jesus Christ.

PRIORITIES

✦ Building character and a relationship with the holy spirit through worship

✦ Nurturing a love for God and growing a desire of the word

✦ Engaging the youth in sharing the great commission of Jesus Christ through evangelism
