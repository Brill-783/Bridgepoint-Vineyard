Finacial  Ministry

VISION

To express the heart of God in the way we manage our money, resources , and property of the church. To serve with Kingdom integrity as we record and deposit all financial giving, pay all financial obligations, and report all financial information to the church Leadership and Congregation of BVCF.





VALUES

✦ OWNERSHIP — To always remember God owns all our resources

✦ POSSESSION — To share our possessions with one another in the Body of Christ

✦ MERCY — To demonstrate compassion and mercy by giving money to the needy

✦ EVANGELISM — extend God’s heart through supporting evangelistic work

✦ MISSION — Develop the heart of missions for Church planting.





PRIORITIES

✦ Always act honestly concerning money

✦ See the Church of Jesus Christ advanced (home and abroad)

✦ See the needs of fellowship met as part of the church obligation

✦ See the needs of fellowship met as part of the church obligation

✦ To effectively manage money so that it will facilitate the greatest possible benefit to the Kingdom of Christ

✦ Live in financial freedom instead of bondage

✦ To budget well, in order to provide for their families and honor God financially.





PRACTICES

✦ Give generously and regularly as a local church and other needs in the Body of Christ

✦ Maintain a solid Christian witness by paying bills on time

✦ Pray about spending and keeping priorities straight according to the Bible instruction

✦ Plan well for the future, but trust only in God each day

✦ Demonstrate a heart of generosity with the church family





PROGRAMS: 2021

A. Training seminar for members on how to budget.

B. Quarterly report

C. Meet to pray for church resources.
