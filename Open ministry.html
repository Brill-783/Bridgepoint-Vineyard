OPEN  ARMS  MINISTRY



VISION

To continue the Kingdom Ministry of Jesus Christ by helping with physical needs and resources to families and individuals in our local church and local community. Matthew 15:32




VALUES

✦ Lovebr
✦ Charity and Hospitality
✦ Compassion and Mercy
✦ Service or servanthood, (Matt 20:25-28)





PRIORITIES



✦ To continue the Kingdom Ministry of Jesus Christ by helping with physical needs and resources to families and individuals in our local church and local community. Matthew 15:32

✦ Ministry to the lost - Ministry to the lost is naturally linked to healing the sick. Often their healing brought them into relationship with God. Evangelism is the centre of all healing.

✦ Ministry to the poor - To be a friend of Jesus is to be a friend of the poor and the oppressed. It goes to the heart of the gospel and the integrity of our witness in the world. (Js 2:1-16).





  PRACTICES

✦ Services - prisons, orphanages, hospices, hospitals, etc.
✦ Sharing - meals and others ✦ Giving (goods, money, talent, gifts, and time)
✦ ‘Ministry time’ - lay hands, pray for people, minister to each other (Eph 4:12;1Pet 2:4,5).
✦ Mobilizing resources to support and enhance human dignity (Matt. 25:35-60).
