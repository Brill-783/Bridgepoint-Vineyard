Kids MINISTRY

VISION

Bringing the children closer to God Mark 10:14; To create an intimate friendship with God from a young age Proverbs 22:6; To facilitate their lifelong walk with God.

MISSION

✦To develop a community of young disciples who know God, and bring light to the world.

VALUES

✦ Bible: the word of God is an important instrument in man's life proverbs 3:2-4..

✦ Communication: it is important to create transparent communication for children. Teaching children to communicate with God is essential..

✦ TGod: exposing children to God’s love and knowing God as the ever present help.|

PRIORITIES

✦ Word: preaching and teaching the word, beliefs and correct doctrines to avoid children from being exploited by the distorted word in the world John 1:1

✦ Reading: the word encouraging children to spend time reading the word for themselves broaden their understanding of God and prompt them to ask more questions about God.

✦ Children's choir: a platform in which children can lead worship

PRACTICES

✦ Evangelism

✦ Planning and hosting church programs

✦ Praying together as women and sharing the word of God

PROGRAMS: 2021

✦ Talent show: children showcase their passion about and talents
✦ Family fun day: An event in which children are allowed to get to bond with their families and friends outside the church environment and work together
✦ Children's camp, a few days on an adventure with friends to know about God a week of revision of all that has been learned that year.
✦ 25 December Christmas service: preparations and the new year resolutions
