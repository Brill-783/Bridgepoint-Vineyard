About Us

Hello and welcome to our fellowship where we seek to follow Jesus and extend God’s Kingdom in our community. We exist to honor our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ, by carrying out his command to make disciples of all nations Matthew 28. Further, we place an emphasis on discipleship as a means to pursue God together, learning from Him to be extraordinary followers of Jesus. Bridgepoint Vineyard stands firmly in the center of orthodox Christian teaching and practice. We draw from the rich traditions of the Church throughout history. We love teaching the Bible because it is our rule of faith and practice.

ABOUT THE VINEYARD MOVEMENT

Bridgepoint Vineyard church is part of the Vineyard Global, a movement network of over 3,000 churches worldwide sharing a common set of values. Founded by the late John Wimber.
