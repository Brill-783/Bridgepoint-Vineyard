Administartion Ministry


VISION

By the Grace of God, to be a God inspired administrative team, supporting the actualization of the vision and strategies of Bridgepoint Vineyard Christian Fellowship. (1 Cor. 12:21; 3 John)



VALUES

✦ Stewardship (Col 4:27, Col 1: 25) What God has entrusted in our hands is not to be mismanaged, but with deep sense of duty of care, we endeavor to appropriate it with love and a sense of great honor

✦ Voluntarism, faithfulness and sacrifice (Rom 15:16). A cheerful and sacrificial service to God in faith and loyalty.

✦ Teamwork and Like-mindedness (Ecclesiastes 2:9, 1 Corinthians 1:10 & 14, Romans 15:5) Supporting one another in doing God’s work, with pure hearts...“Everyone gets to play”.

✦ Integrity. Honesty and trustworthiness before God.



PRIORITIES

✦ Mobilizing and the management of the Church’s operational assets.

✦ Safety during church program.

✦ Church Programs:- Church activities are a vital tool guiding Church growth, therefore they need to be implemented with care and excellence.

✦ The sanctuary provides the place of worship for believers to express their faith and love for God.

✦ The Pastor’s Office and its wellbeing, facilitating the preaching of the full Gospel of God and enriching as well as grounding believers in faith.
